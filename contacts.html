Common Questions

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year for medical or travel reasons. Please visit the front desk to apply.

Do you offer day passes?

Absolutely! We offer a 1-day pass for $15, which is fully refundable if you decide to sign up for a monthly membership within 24 hours.

Are personal trainers included?

Personal trainers are an add-on service, although our Premium Membership includes 2 free sessions per month. Ask the front desk for trainer rates.
